About company

Movie Booking System is a comprehensive platform designed to simplify the process of booking movie tickets online. Users can browse through a variety of theatres and movies, view showtimes, and book tickets with ease.

The system includes beautifully designed templates for a seamless user experience, including a centered and stylish login form and a personalized profile page displaying user information and booked tickets. Admins can manage theatres, movies, shows, and customer details efficiently, ensuring a smooth operation for both users and administrators.
